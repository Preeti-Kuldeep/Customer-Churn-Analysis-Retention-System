# Customer Churn Analysis & Retention System

## Project Overview

Customer Churn Analysis & Retention System is an end-to-end Data Analytics project designed to identify customers who are likely to leave a company's services and analyze the factors contributing to customer churn.

The project helps businesses improve customer retention, reduce revenue loss, and make data-driven decisions using SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI.

---

## Business Problem

Customer retention is critical for business growth. Acquiring a new customer is often more expensive than retaining an existing one. Organizations lose customers due to service issues, pricing concerns, lack of engagement, and competition.

This project analyzes customer behavior, churn patterns, revenue impact, and customer complaints to support effective retention strategies.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer churn trends
* Calculate churn rate
* Measure revenue loss
* Identify high-risk customers
* Analyze customer complaints
* Generate business KPIs

## Database Design

The project consists of four main tables:

1. Customers
2. Subscriptions
3. Payments
4. Complaints

Relationships:

* Customers → Subscriptions (1:M)
* Customers → Payments (1:M)
* Customers → Complaints (1:M)
