# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

Customer Churn Analysis & Retention System

## Business Problem

Many organizations lose customers every month due to poor service, pricing issues, lack of engagement, or better offers from competitors. Without proper analysis, companies cannot identify which customers are at risk of leaving, resulting in revenue loss and reduced customer satisfaction.

## Project Objectives

1. Analyze customer churn trends.
2. Calculate customer churn rate.
3. Measure revenue loss due to churn.
4. Identify high-risk customers.
5. Analyze customer complaints.
6. Generate business reports and dashboards.
7. Support data-driven decision making.

## Project Scope

### In Scope

* Customer Analysis
* Subscription Analysis
* Payment Analysis
* Complaint Analysis
* KPI Reporting
* Excel Dashboard
* SQL Analysis
* Python Analysis

### Out of Scope

* Machine Learning Models
* Real-Time Analytics
* Web Applications

## Expected Outcomes

* Identify churned customers.
* Measure revenue loss.
* Improve retention strategies.
* Generate actionable business insights.
* Support business decision making.
